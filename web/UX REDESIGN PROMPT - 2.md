1. Sidebar — Feels Static & Plain

The sidebar is a flat white panel with plain text links and no visual energy
Fix: Add a subtle frosted-glass or tinted background, a soft left-border glow on the active item (not just a background wash), smooth collapse animation to icon-only mode, and a more refined CasaAI logo treatment at the top (consider a gradient or animated mark)

2. Dashboard Is Not a Dashboard — It's a Listings Page

The "/dashboard" route currently just shows the Annunci (Listings) page. There's no actual command-center overview
Fix: Build a real dashboard as the landing page: a bento-grid layout with KPI cards (listings, contacts, appointments today, AI content generated), a mini calendar preview, recent activity feed, and quick-action buttons. The Annunci page should be a separate route

3. Stat Cards (Top Row) — Too Uniform and Flat

The 4 metric cards (10 Annunci, 14 Clienti, 40 App., 2 AI) are identically sized, plain white boxes with colored circle icons. There's zero visual hierarchy
Fix: Use a bento-grid with varied card sizes (the most important metric gets a larger card). Add subtle gradient backgrounds or glass effects. Include mini spark-lines or trend arrows. The AI content card especially should stand out with a shimmer/glow treatment

4. Property Cards — Need Premium Real Estate Energy

Cards are functional but feel like database records. The placeholder house icons for listings without photos look particularly cheap
Fix: Make image area taller, use gradient overlays on images for text contrast, add a glass-effect price badge (not just a plain red pill), show status with a color-coded ribbon or chip (not just a small "Bozza" text badge). For listings without photos, use a sophisticated gradient placeholder with the property type icon, not a plain beige box

5. Contact Cards — Visually Cluttered, No Hierarchy

Every card shows the same info at the same visual weight. The colored avatar circles are the only differentiation
Fix: Lead with the name prominently, de-emphasize secondary info (phone, email) into a hover-reveal or subtle row. Add a type-colored left border accent instead of just a badge. The budget range and location should use a lighter visual treatment

6. Calendar Page — Color Coding is Confusing

Multiple colored dots (blue, red, green, orange, purple) with no legend explaining what they mean. The right sidebar appointment list is cramped
Fix: Add a clear color legend, use softer appointment blocks (rounded, with subtle fill matching the agent color), give the right panel more breathing room, add a smooth animation when switching weeks

7. Campaigns Page — Empty State Waste

Only one campaign showing. The massive empty white space below communicates "abandoned product"
Fix: Add a compelling empty state: an illustration or gradient card saying "Launch your first AI-powered email campaign" with a prominent CTA. When campaigns exist, show analytics preview (open rate, click rate) inline

8. Pricing Page — Functional but Forgettable

Standard 3-column pricing cards. The "Più popolare" badge looks like an afterthought
Fix: Give the popular plan a gradient border or subtle glow effect. Add animated background on hover. The monthly/annual toggle could have a smoother pill-slider animation. Consider making the popular plan physically larger

9. Global: No Animations Anywhere

Every page transition is instant with no easing. Cards appear all at once. Nothing reacts to hover or scroll
Fix: Add staggered fade-in on page load (cards cascade in with 50ms delays), smooth hover lift on all cards, page-level fade transitions on route change, and skeleton shimmer loaders

10. Global: The AI Identity Is Nearly Invisible

The small red "✦ AI" badge on listing cards is the only indication this is an AI-powered product
Fix: Create a distinctive AI visual language: a gradient shimmer button for all AI actions, a breathing glow animation when AI is processing, a "Generated by CasaAI" badge with a sparkle icon, and subtle AI-themed ambient effects (like a faint aurora gradient) on sections where AI is active

11. Global: Typography Lacks Character

The current Geist font is clean but generic — it's literally the Vercel starter template default
Fix: Switch to a distinctive display font for headings (Clash Display, Satoshi, or Cabinet Grotesk) to create visual identity. Use more dramatic size contrast between headings and body text
